About this newsletter

What We're Building is a bi-monthly product update written directly by Rick Johnston, CPO at Quorum. Each issue covers the latest developments across all of Quorum's product lines — MORI, Quorum DMS, DealerMine, Autovance, and Accessible Accessories — so you always know what's shipping, what's coming, and why it matters to your business.

The newsletter goes out at the start of each month. The content is the same for everyone; it's simply organized to lead with the products most relevant to you. If you received a direct link from Rick, it's already set up that way.
